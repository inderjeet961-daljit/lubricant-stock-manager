<original_problem_statement>
Build and deploy a complete Android mobile application for Automotive Lubricant Stock Management.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**

**Users &amp; Roles:**
- **Owner/Sales:** A single unified user who can view a combined dashboard, add new items (raw material, packing, finished goods), set manufacturing recipes, take stock from the factory into their car, record sales, and send returns to the factory (creating a pending return). They cannot directly alter factory stock.
- **Factory Manager:** Can add raw material stock, manufacture loose oil, pack finished goods, approve incoming returns, mark damaged packing material, and handle leakage. They cannot create new item types.

**Key Features:**
- **Unified Owner Dashboard:** With a global search bar and sections for Finished Goods (Factory vs. Car), Loose Oil, Raw Materials, and Packing Materials.
- **Owner Actions:** Take Stock in Car, Record Sales (from Car, Transport, Direct Dispatch), Return to Factory (pending approval).
- **Scalable Item Creation:** Owner can add new raw materials, packing materials, and finished products with linked components.
- **Manufacturing Recipes:** Owner sets recipes (must sum to 100%). When the manager manufactures, raw materials are auto-deducted.
- **Factory Manager Actions:** Add Raw Material, Manufacture Loose Oil, Pack Finished Goods, Approve Returns (Drain &amp; Reuse or Scrap), Mark Damaged Packing.
- **Data Safety:** Timestamped actions, no negative stock, prevent duplicate item names.
- **Optional Features:** Low stock alerts, end-of-day summary.
- **Deployment:** A fully working Android app with two roles and a live cloud database.
</original_problem_statement>
