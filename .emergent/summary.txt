<original_problem_statement>
Build and deploy a complete Android mobile application for Automotive Lubricant Stock Management.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS (Initial):**
- **Users &amp; Roles:** Owner/Sales and Factory Manager with distinct permissions.
- **Key Features:** Dashboards, stock management (sales, transfers, manufacturing, packing), item and recipe creation, and a return approval workflow.
- **Data Safety:** Timestamped actions, no negative stock, prevent duplicate item names.
- **Deployment:** A fully working Android app.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS (Evolved during session):**
- **CRUD for all Items:** The owner should be able to edit and delete all item types (raw materials, packing materials, loose oils, finished goods).
- **Manager Stock Input:** The manager must be able to add stock for both raw materials and packing materials.
- **Accurate Packing Logic:** Packing a finished good (e.g., Thrive 900ml) must deduct the correct fractional amount (0.9L) from the corresponding loose oil stock.
- **Accurate Stock Updates:** All stock operations (packing, sales, transfers) for finished goods must correctly identify items by both **name AND pack size** to prevent overwriting stock of a different size (e.g., updating Thrive 900ml should not affect Thrive 1L).
- **Accountability via Reporting:** The owner needs a Reports screen to view a log of all transactions from the last 7 days to track activity and reconcile stock.
- **Intermediate Goods Manufacturing (NEW):** A new workflow is required where some items (e.g., 'VI' and 'VI Super') are manufactured from raw materials, and then this manufactured item becomes a 'raw material' for subsequent blending/manufacturing processes.
- **Backup/Deployment Guide (NEW):** The user wants a document explaining how to back up the app and data.
</original_problem_statement>
